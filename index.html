How the calculation works

For every trackpoint, the tool computes cumulative ascent by adding only positive elevation deltas. It then uses a two-pointer scan to find the shortest time window where cumulative ascent increases by at least the target. Example: +800 m, -100 m, +200 m counts as 1000 m cumulative ascent.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your GPX file is not uploaded to a server.
