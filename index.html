Calculation details

The tool computes cumulative ascent by adding only positive elevation changes between consecutive GPX points. For example, +800 m, -100 m, +200 m counts as 1000 m of ascent.

It then scans the activity with two pointers to find the shortest elapsed-time window where cumulative ascent increases by at least the selected target.
